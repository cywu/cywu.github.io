Reseach

I am most interested in Number Theory and Arithmetic Geometry. Currently I am focusing on automorphic forms, theta correspondence, L-functions, endoscopy, periods and Abelian varieties.

Miscellaneous

  • My Firefox extension to make downloading academic papers easier in the University of Melbourne or potentially other universities as well. When you land on a page with the article that you want to read and hit a paywall, just click the button of this extension instead of dejectedly going to the library site and starting search all over. You will see the paywall gone and the article can be downloaded if you are logged in with your university already. Or you will get taken to the log-in page of the university if you are not logged in yet. In this case, log in to get redirected to the article automatically. Please let me know if it works for you or not. Source code here. I'm obviously not very decided with the name of the extension.
